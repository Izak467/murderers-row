How to Play
  • Pick an era, then each round the slot machine spins a random MLB team + year from that era's window. Don't like what you got? You have one team skip and one year skip per game.
  • Browse the full roster of everyone who suited up that season. Stat cards are color-coded red → green so the studs stand out. Badge guide: 🏆 MVP · 🐣 Rookie of the Year · ⚡ Silver Slugger · ⭐ All-Star.
  • A player needs 10+ games at a position to be eligible there.
  • Fill all 9 slots: C · 1B · 2B · 3B · SS · LF · CF · RF · DH. Use Available Only or the Position filter to find the right fits fast.
  • Slots lock once filled — but you get one ↔ Move Token to reassign any placed player to another open position they qualify for.
  • Scored on AVG, OBP, SLG, HR, RBI, R, and SB — era-adjusted, weighted by games played. Durability counts. 162-0 takes elite production everywhere.
