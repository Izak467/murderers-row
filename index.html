Murderers' Row
Can you go 162-0?
Build the most feared lineup in baseball history · 2005–2025
How to Play
  • Each round, a slot machine spins a random MLB team + year (2005–2025, excluding 2020).
  • You see the full roster — everyone who played — sorted by games. Pick any player, assign them to any open position they're eligible for.
  • A player needs 10+ games at a position that season to be eligible there.
  • Fill all 9 slots: C · 1B · 2B · 3B · SS · LF · CF · RF · DH.
  • Filled slots are locked. You have one Move token to reassign placed players to different eligible positions.
  • You also have one team skip and one year skip per game.
  • Lineup is scored on AVG / OBP / SLG + HR, RBI, R, SB. Rate stats are adjusted for era and games played — a full season in a pitcher's park counts differently than 60 games in 2006. The win curve is brutal — 162-0 requires excellence in every category.
