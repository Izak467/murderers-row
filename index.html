How to Play
The Goal

Build the strongest 9-man lineup you can from players across MLB history. Your lineup gets scored — the higher the better.

Each Round
  • A slot machine spins a random MLB team + year from your era. See the full roster, pick any player, and drop them into an open eligible position.
  • A player needs 10+ games at a position that season to qualify there.
  • You get one team skip and one year skip per game — use them wisely.
Your Lineup
  • 9 slots to fill: C · 1B · 2B · 3B · SS · LF · CF · RF · DH
  • Use Available Only or the Position filter to find fits fast.
  • Slots lock once filled. One ↔ Move Token lets you reassign any placed player to another open position they qualify for.
Player Badges
  • MVP
  • Rookie of the Year
  • Silver Slugger
  • All-Star

Stat cards are color-coded red → green — the studs stand out at a glance.

⚡ Basic Strategy
  • Games played matter. A star who played 150 games scores higher than the same player in 60, even at a better rate. Durability counts.
  • Volume wins. HR, RBI, Runs, and SB are raw totals across all 9 players — pack in counting stats wherever you can.
  • Know your era. Scoring weights shift between eras — speed matters more in the nostalgia era, power dominates the steroid era.
  • Don't neglect your corners. Catcher and middle infield count as much as your cleanup hitters. Weak links hurt.
